CampusMint helps students think before spending and save for later life.

Features

Built to help students slow down, stay organized, and protect their future.

02

Future Safety Goals

How it works

Simple money habits now can create more freedom and safety later.

Step 1

Set up your student life

Add your income, support, rent, food, transport, and school costs to build a realistic monthly picture.

Step 2

Pause before purchases

Use quick prompts that ask what the purchase is for and whether it helps now, later, or neither.

Step 3

Grow your cushion

Weekly reflections help students save more consistently for emergencies and early adult life after college.
